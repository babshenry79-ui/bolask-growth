FAQ

Questions
Answered Honestly

How long does it take to see results? +
Most clients see initial CTR improvements within 4–8 weeks of applying the thumbnail and title changes. SEO-driven results typically compound over 60–90 days. This isn't a magic fix — it's a strategy that requires consistent execution.
Is the $70 a flat rate? +
$70 is the starting price for a basic channel audit. The final price depends on your channel's size, the number of videos reviewed, and the scope of work. I'll give you a clear quote after a quick look at your channel — no surprises.
Do you create content for me? +
No. I provide the strategy, direction, and optimization — but you create the content. Think of me as your growth strategist, not a content creator or editor.
What if my channel is brand new? +
New channels can absolutely benefit from working together — we'll build the right foundation from the start so you don't waste months going in the wrong direction. Starting with the right strategy is far more valuable than fixing mistakes later.
Do you guarantee results? +
I don't make guarantees because results depend on how consistently you execute the strategy. What I do guarantee is a clear, well-researched, honest plan tailored to your channel — and full support as you implement it. Every creator I've worked with who followed through has seen real improvement.
How do we communicate? +
Primarily through Discord and email. After onboarding, you'll have access to my Discord for questions and check-ins. All deliverables are sent via email.
Do you work with Twitch streamers too? +
Yes — Twitch growth strategy is one of my core services. I can work with streamers looking to grow on Twitch alone, or help creators use YouTube and Twitch together as a powerful cross-platform growth system.
